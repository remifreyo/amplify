Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, reach out to us directly.

📅 What's your booking process?

Book a free consultation, we discuss your project, provide a quote, you approve and pay the deposit (usually 50%), we schedule your session, deliver your files within 3-7 days.

💰 What are your rates?

Rates vary by service type and project scope. Studio rental starts at $85/hr. Full production packages start around $500. Contact us for a custom quote based on your needs.

📸 Do you provide raw files?

We deliver fully edited, high-resolution files. Raw files are available for an additional fee. All deliverables include usage rights for your business purposes.

🎙️ Can I record a podcast remotely?

Yes! We offer remote podcast recording with our engineers. We can also help you set up remote guests while you record in-studio for the best quality.

⏱️ How long does post-production take?

Standard turnaround is 3-7 business days for most projects. Rush delivery (24-48 hours) is available for an additional fee. Web development projects typically take 2-4 weeks.

🎬 Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! We're available for on-location shoots in the DMV area and beyond. Travel fees may apply depending on distance and logistics.

👥 Can I bring my own team?

Absolutely. You're welcome to bring your own hair/makeup artists, stylists, or creative director. We can also provide a full crew if needed.

🔄 What's your revision policy?

Most packages include 2 rounds of minor revisions. Major changes or additional rounds may incur extra fees. We work with you to get it right.
